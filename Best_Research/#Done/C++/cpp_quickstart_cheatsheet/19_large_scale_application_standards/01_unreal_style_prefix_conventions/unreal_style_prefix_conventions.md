# unreal_style_prefix_conventions.cpp

## InDepth

Unreal-style naming is a team convention to make large codebases easier to scan:

- `A*`: actor-like world entities
- `U*`: engine-managed object types
- `I*`: interfaces
- `F*`: struct/value types
- `E*`: enums
- `T*`: templates

The important part is consistency inside one codebase.

If your team adopts prefixes, keep them stable and documented.
